# 05 — ANALYSIS: Brand, Identity & Consistency

> Assignment for Gemini. You have read `00-CONTEXT.md`. Analyze **brand identity, voice, and
> cross-surface consistency only**. Standard response format. This is the "does it feel like one
> confident product, by one hand?" dimension.

## Scope
- **Wordmark & naming:** "VFX Syntech", byline "Created by State", tagline "AI-Powered.
  Node-Based. Limitless." Gold "Syntech" in a Space Grotesk wordmark.
- **Logo mark:** a diamond containing "VS" (gold, rotated 45°, glow) in the sidebar.
- **Voice/tone:** the app's copy is sci-fi/poetic/technical — "Obsidian Constellation Vault",
  "neural nodes", "Accessing vault index…", "ENGINE STANDBY // CONSTALLATION GRAPH SLEEPING",
  "Master MP4", "SynEngine". The embedded AI is styled as a "Vault Oracle".
- **Consistency problem:** three visual worlds coexist (audit): the restrained obsidian-gold
  **shell**; the unbranded **node rainbow**; and the **iridescent** effect iframes (Barlow
  Condensed, gold→blue→violet→pink glows, gel/glass). Plus a warm/cool temperature split and a
  gold-vs-violet accent duality.

## Questions to answer (be specific)
1. **Identity verdict:** in one paragraph, what *is* the VFX Syntech brand today, and is it
   coherent? Score its identity strength /10 and name the one thing most diluting it.
2. **Wordmark & logo:** critique the "VS" diamond and the wordmark. Do they read premium? Propose
   concrete refinements (or a redesign brief) — construction, weight, spacing, the gold treatment,
   how the mark scales from 44px sidebar to a favicon. Should "Created by State" stay, and how
   should it be set so it reads as a signature, not clutter?
3. **The "obsidian / vault / constellation" metaphor:** is it an asset or a costume? Recommend
   how to make the metaphor *earn* its place visually and verbally, or how to tone it down if it
   reads as try-hard. Tie visual motifs (nodes, gold, deep space) to the metaphor deliberately.
4. **Accent identity:** gold = brand, violet = AI. Is that a *meaningful* semantic system worth
   keeping (warm human tool vs. cool AI), or an accident to unify? Give the rule for what each
   color "means" so it's used consistently, or the plan to collapse to one.
5. **Cross-surface unification (priority):** propose how to reconcile the three visual worlds.
   Should the iframe effects be pulled into the shell language, or kept as intentionally "hotter"
   immersive worlds with a *defined* relationship (e.g. "effects run hot, the console stays
   cool")? Give the governing principle either way, and the shared tokens/type that must carry
   across so it feels like one product.
6. **Voice & microcopy:** critique the sci-fi tone. Where is it delightful vs. where does it hurt
   usability (e.g. "CONSTALLATION" typo, dev-flavored standby text)? Give a voice guideline (3–4
   rules) + rewrites of 5 key strings (empty states, loading, errors, the standby screen, the
   AI panel intro) that keep character while reading premium and correct.
7. **Signature moments:** every premium product has 2–3 "signature" details people screenshot.
   Identify VFX Syntech's best candidates (the constellation? the analog clock? the gold diamond?
   the node flow?) and how to elevate them into deliberate brand signatures.
8. **Iconography & favicon/OG:** is the lucide icon set on-brand? Recommend stroke/size norms, and
   specify a favicon + social-share (OG) image direction consistent with the identity.

## Deliverable
- An **identity summary** (what the brand is / should be, in 3 sentences) + strength score.
- A **wordmark/logo** critique with concrete refinement specs.
- A **cross-surface consistency principle** (the rule that unifies shell + nodes + effects).
- A **voice guideline** (3–4 rules) + 5 rewritten strings.
- 2–3 nominated **signature moments** with elevation specs.
- The prioritized Recommendations table (Change → Why → Spec → Effort → Impact).
